REQUESTING PTO (Paid Time Off)
Employees must submit PTO requests at least 3 business days in advance through the HR portal. 
Requests over 5 consecutive business days require manager approval.

IT EQUIPMENT REQUESTS
Employees needing laptops, monitors, or accessories must submit an IT ticket. 
Replacement hardware requires approval from the manager and IT asset team.

REPORTING WORKPLACE ISSUES
Employees should first talk to their manager. If unresolved, submit a confidential report through the Ethics Hotline.

INTERNAL TRANSFERS
Employees are eligible for transfer after 6 months in role. Manager notification is required prior to applying.

TIMESHEET CORRECTIONS
Employees must submit corrections by Friday 5 PM. Managers approve corrections before payroll processes.
