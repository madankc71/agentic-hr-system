FULL-TIME EMPLOYEES
Eligible for PTO and 401k after 60 days of employment.

PART-TIME EMPLOYEES
Eligible for prorated PTO after 6 months.

CONTRACTORS
Not eligible for PTO, bonuses, or company-paid benefits. Eligibility depends on vendor agreements.

INTERNS
Not eligible for PTO or bonuses unless stated in the internship contract.

REMOTE WORK ELIGIBILITY
Remote work eligibility is determined by the role and manager approval.

EXCEPTIONS
HR may review exceptions on a case-by-case basis.
