Code of Conduct

Employees are expected to act with integrity, honesty, and professionalism at all times. 
Harassment, discrimination, or abusive behavior is strictly prohibited.

Workplace Behavior

Employees should respect colleagues, collaborate, and communicate respectfully. 
Inappropriate language, bullying, or threatening actions will not be tolerated.

Confidentiality

Employees must protect company data, customer information, and internal documents. 
Sharing confidential information outside the organization is prohibited unless authorized.

Reporting Concerns

Employees are encouraged to report conduct concerns to HR or their manager. 
Reports may be submitted anonymously and will be investigated without retaliation.
